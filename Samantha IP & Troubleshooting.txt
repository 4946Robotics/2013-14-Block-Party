Procedure for connecting the Sammy:
  With the FTC_Pit network set up, and everything off:
  1: Connect to the FTC_Pit network (See Problem 2 if the wifi light is blinking slowly)
  2: Turn on the NXT and flip the on/off switch to "ON"
  3: Start up the program "samofcs.exe" (field control system)
  4: Click "OK" in the upper right hand corner of the samofcs window
  5: Click "Choose" in 'current teams'
  6: Click on 4946
  7: Click connect under 'current teams'
  8: Hope


Troubleshooting problems:

Problem 1: Can't connect to the Sammy
  1. If the Sammy is frozen and won't turn off, remove the battery and put it back in.
  2. Are you running the program? (Make sure you're running teleop or autonomous)
  3. Turn the NXT off and on
  4. Close out of everthing, disconnect from the Sammy. Turn the robot off and on, then reconnect.
  5. Hold down the red button on the Sammy, and try to reconnect

Problem 2: The wifi light is blinking slowly
  1. Go to the FTC_Pit wifi
  2. Go to status
  3. Go to properties (NOT "Wireless Properties")
  4. Go to the IPV4 (NOT IPV6)
  5. Type in the IP adress, subnet mask, and default gateway values listed above
  6. Pray
Problem 3:  NXT & power lights are on, but can't connect with computer
  1. Swap out which computer you're using to connect
